Common questions

What exactly do you do? +
I help B2B SaaS teams use AI to drive pipeline - three ways: I run hands-on workshops that teach your GTM team to build AI tools and automations, I build custom agents and tools for you, and I share the playbooks and tools through my communities. The goal is always the same: more pipeline with a smaller team.
Who is the workshop for? +
Marketing, demand gen, ABM and sales teams at B2B SaaS companies - technical background not required. We work on your real GTM challenges, and your team leaves with a roadmap and something live.
Do we need engineers to build these tools? +
No. I build with Claude Code & Cowork, which let non-engineers ship working tools. That's the whole point - your marketers become builders.
How fast can you build a custom tool? +
Typically 1-3 weeks from spec to a shipped, working product - not a 6-month roadmap.
How do we get started? +
Book a short call. We'll figure out whether a workshop, a custom build or a quick consultation is the right first step for your team.
